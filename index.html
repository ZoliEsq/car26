About

The inspiration for Car26Photography

  • 1993-1997

    Starting Small

    Matt's passion of the Fire Department originated with his godfather, Joseph C Watkins, who was a member of the Plainville Fire Department from 1969 until he retired in 1997.

  • 2005-2010

    Interest Builds

    Matt became a Fire Cadet in the Town of Farmington CT at age 12 and was with them until he was 17 years old. During this time Matt attended numerous events within the Fire department, including training drills, parades, department functions, and much more.

  • July 2012

    Things Change

    Matt Joined The Plainville Fire Company, where he loved being a part of a great organization but he had to take a step back from the live fire action.

  • September 2016

    Car26 is born!

    Matt became a member of the CT Fire Photographers Association, and started photographing for fire houses. Photography is something he loves to do because he enjoys having the ability to capture a special moment in a photograph. This has started off very small, but has grown into something much larger and has became his passion. With the help of a good friend, DJ Voisine, the decision was made to photograph professionally.
